About the Project

This project was developed by a student team over the course of a semester and is designed to educate users about climate change in a fun and engaging way. Climate change is a serious issue that affects us all, and it is crucial for everyone to learn about its causes, impacts, and solutions. We hope this website provides valuable insights while keeping the learning experience enjoyable. If you have any suggestions, problems, or comments, please feel free to email us at igniteteam@gmail.com.

Thank you for visiting our website!
